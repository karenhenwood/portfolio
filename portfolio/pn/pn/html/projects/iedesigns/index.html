About

I.E. Designs is the result of collaborative efforts between artist Evan ESK Wilson and designer Isabel Solomon.

Each hand manufactured custom piece exemplifies the aesthetic that will be carried forward into new lighting elements from this exciting new collaboration.

The first line of elegant luminaire prototypes are presented to the public for purchase with love and intention in dark times.
